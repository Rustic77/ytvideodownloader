How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

Cookies

We use cookies for Google Analytics and Google AdSense. These help us understand how visitors use our site and display relevant advertising. You can control cookies through your browser settings. To opt out of personalized advertising, visit Google Ad Settings.

Data Retention

Downloaded video files are automatically deleted from our servers within 1 hour or immediately after download, whichever comes first. We do not store your videos permanently. Download tokens expire after one use or one hour.

Third-Party Services

  • YouTube: For video information retrieval and downloading
